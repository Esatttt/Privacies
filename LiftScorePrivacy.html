Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 20, 2025

Lift Score is a fully offline application. We do not collect, store, process, sell, or share any personal data or usage data.

Data Collection

We collect no data—personal or otherwise. The app does not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or any third-party SDKs that transmit data.

Internet & Network Access

The app does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests. All functionality is available offline.

Local-Only Operation

Any content you create or configure within the app remains on your device. We do not have access to it.

Children’s Privacy

Because the app does not collect any data, there is no personal information gathered from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the app works (for example, adding online features), we will update this policy and the “Last updated” date above.
